My Story

Prior to starting the Bachelor of Information Technology I was a Physiotherapist. I decided to make the jump to Information Technology because I wanted the skills to be able to develop any ideas that came to mind. Project 1 has been a great experience in that we are finally working with interesting technology and I am confident in my ability to work with difficult code. I have thoroughly enjoyed learning about APIs, backend coding, virtual reality, and android development. It has solidified my understanding of where I want to work and what I want to work with. My confidence has drastically improved over the course of this semester, and I feel that I can tackle any problem head on.
